About Clark's

Clark's Gym is a fitness center located in Pulaski, TN and Lawrenceburg, TN. We are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to meet all of your fitness needs. Clark's Gym offers multiple fitness classes hosted by the best instructors. We also have personal trainers on-site that will help get you grow stronger and get fit! We offer different memberships to fit your needs.
